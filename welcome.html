Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this app for?

For people who value a clean home but feel overwhelmed. If you've been stuck in a cycle of weekend cleaning marathons followed by weeks of guilt, this system is for you.

How long does each task take?

Most daily tasks take 5-10 minutes. The goal is to make cleaning so quick that you actually do it consistently.

What if I miss a day?

Life happens. The app won't punish you - it simply picks up where you left off. The system is designed to be forgiving while still encouraging consistency.

Can I skip or swap a task?

Yes. If today's task doesn't work for you, you can skip it and it will cycle back around eventually.

How often will each room be cleaned?

It depends on how many rooms you have, but the system ensures every room cycles through all three pillars regularly.
